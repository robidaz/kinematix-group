Team,

I'm excited to share the kickoff of an initiative that's been on our roadmap for some time: **VendorVault** — KineMatix Group's centralized vendor intelligence platform.

Over the last several quarters we've heard the same theme echoed across nearly every business unit: **we don't have a single, trustworthy place to understand who our vendors are, what they cost, how they perform, and which ones to consider for a given client need.** Sales teams build their own spreadsheets. Procurement maintains a different system of record. Engineering teams source vendors organically through individual relationships. The result is duplicated effort, missed renewal opportunities, and — most importantly — a slower path to the right answer when a client asks us a question.

VendorVault is our answer to that.

### What VendorVault delivers

- **A unified vendor catalog** spanning the ~250+ partners and prospective vendors we track today, with consistent metadata for type, cost, reputation, contract status, and specializations.
- **AI-assisted vendor matching.** Describe a client's need in plain English ("mid-range cybersecurity vendor with strong compliance support") and the system narrows the catalog down to the strongest fits — no manual filtering required.
- **Renewal and spend visibility** at a glance, so account leads can stay ahead of expiring contracts and sourcing teams can negotiate from data, not anecdote.
- **A single source of truth** that our solutions architects, account executives, and procurement leads can all rely on.

### Why now

Our practice has scaled meaningfully over the last 18 months, and our vendor relationships have grown alongside it. Continuing to operate without a shared system creates friction at exactly the moment we need to move faster. VendorVault is a deliberate investment in operational leverage — a small, focused product that removes a recurring tax on every client engagement.

### What's next

This week we're rolling out an initial preview to the solutions and procurement teams. The data set is intentionally a mix of real partners and simulated entries during this phase — we'll be progressively replacing simulated records with curated, verified data over the next quarter. Please treat the metrics, contract values, and renewal dates as illustrative until we communicate otherwise.

If you have feedback, edge cases you'd like the AI matching to handle, or vendor relationships that should be reflected in the catalog, please reach out directly to the VendorVault working group.

Thank you for the work you do every day to deliver for our clients. I'm looking forward to seeing what this unlocks.
